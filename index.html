Our offerings

Attendance Management System (AMS) with PetaScan/PetaTag

A robust, customizable attendance management system designed to fit various institutional and organizational needs, including schools and colleges of any scale. Key features include:

  • Biometric Solutions: PetaScan for Reliable and seamless methods for attendance tracking.
  • RFID solutions: PetaTag for RFID based contactless Attendance marking and more !
  • Scalability: Flexible options to suit small institutions or large enterprises.

Tech Fusion – Shaping Bright Futures

Our sub-brand, Tech Fusion, specializes in providing:

  • Skill-Oriented Internships: Covering topics like web development, digital electronics, Verilog, and more. Certificates provided upon completion.
  • Online Tutoring: Expert-led tuition for Core Electronics, Computer Programming, Engineering Mathematics, and more.

PetaParking – Parking Automation & Management System

A comprehensive solution for parking lot owners, streamlining lot management, ANPR, automated slip generation, and finance tracking.

  • Real-Time Management: Efficiently monitor parking spaces.
  • Advanced ANPR: Automatically capture vehicle plates.
  • Automated Slips: Instant, accurate slip generation.
  • Finance Integration: Seamlessly track revenues and expenses.
